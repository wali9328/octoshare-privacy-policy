Last updated: September 2026

1. Introduction

OctoShare is a utility application that allows users to voluntarily share unused internet bandwidth in exchange for rewards. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By installing, accessing, or using OctoShare, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Developer Information

  • Developer: AG Software House

3. Information We Collect

OctoShare may collect the following types of data:

  • Network usage data (amount of bandwidth shared)
  • Device and technical information (device type, OS version, app version)
  • App usage information related to sharing status

We do not collect personal content such as messages, photos, contacts, or browsing history.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable and manage bandwidth sharing
  • To calculate earnings and rewards
  • To improve app performance and stability
  • To prevent fraud or misuse

5. Third-Party Services

OctoShare integrates third-party services, including but not limited to the Pawns.app SDK, to facilitate secure bandwidth sharing and reward calculation.

These services may process technical and network-related data strictly for operational purposes. No personal content is accessed or shared.

6. Foreground and Background Services

OctoShare may use foreground or background services to enable continuous bandwidth sharing. When active, a persistent notification may be displayed to inform users that sharing is running.

Users can stop sharing at any time from within the app.

7. User Consent

Before using OctoShare, users are prompted to review and accept this Privacy Policy. By enabling bandwidth sharing, you confirm your consent to data processing as described here.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. All communications are encrypted where applicable.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the app.

10. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop bandwidth sharing at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent by discontinuing app use

To exercise these rights, contact us at wali9328@gmail.com.

11. International Data Processing

Your data may be processed outside your country of residence depending on service infrastructure. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

12. Children’s Privacy

OctoShare is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, notified in-app.
